file naming conventions: 

(object type)-(location)-(property)-(state)-(color)

eg. 

wall-bathroom-door-closed-y

is a wall object
in the bathroom
with a door
that is closed
and it's part of the yellow colorset. 

If a section is irrelevant it's just skipped (eg. floor-bathroom-y doesn't have a property or a state) 

locations:
1. attic
2. basement
3. bathroom
4. bedroom
5. kitchen
6. living room

colors (represented in 10s column):
0x. blue
1x. yellow
2x. red

Doors: 1x
